WRIP - Wrist-Mounted Wearable

2024
Hardware / KiCad

Ultra-compact wearable at just 8 grams. Custom flex PCB, breath sensor, LiPo protection, USB-C fast charging, and Apple Watch-style exchangeable straps.

Ask about this project View on GitHub

WRIP is an ultra-miniaturized wrist-mounted wearable device weighing just 8 grams. The custom flex PCB integrates an 8-bit MCU, breath-activated sensor, 58kHz PWM power regulation, and LiPo battery with full protection circuitry.

USB-C fast charging takes the device from 0 to 100% in 40 minutes. The Apple Watch-style strap system allows easy band swapping. Complete hardware design from schematic through PCB layout to enclosure.
